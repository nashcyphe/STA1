The Star Trek: Armada manual is on this disk in Adobe® Acrobat® (.pdf) format. Adobe Acrobat Reader needs to be installed to view this manual that is located on this CD. To install the Adobe Acrobat Reader, open your Star Trek : Armada CD-Rom drive and view the folders. Open the Preview folder. Inside this Preview is a folder called Adobe. Open this folder and double click on the ar405eng.exe icon. Follow the instructions to install the Adobe reader. Once it is installed click here to view the Star Trek Armada manual , or double click on the Armada.pdf inside the help folder.
