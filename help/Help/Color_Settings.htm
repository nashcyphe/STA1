Color Settings

To Change Your Color Setting:

1. Place your mouse cursor over an empty area of your desktop and right-click. At the bottom of the list that appears, click Properties. This will bring up the Display Properties window. You can also bring this window up by clicking the Display icon in the Control Panel.

2. Choose the Settings tab and change the color setting to 16-Bit Color as shown in the Color palette box above.

If you have a video card that supports 16-Bit Color, but you cannot select this as an option, check to see that you have the most updated Windows 95/98 video drivers.
