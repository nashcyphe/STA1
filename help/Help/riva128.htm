Riva 128    

If you are using a video card that has the Riva 128 chipset, you may encounter the following problems:

  • Blurry text.
  • Pixelation.
  • Grainy smoke and fire effects.

As Star Trek: Armada™ does not officially support cards with this chipset, we are unable to respond to customer service issues resulting from use of Star Trek: Armada™ with this chipset.  We do however, strongly recommend that you verify you are using the latest drivers, as this may be able to improve your gameplay performance.

Star Trek: Armada™ defaults to the drivers available from the nVidia website. Please make sure you have the latest drivers from http://www.nvidia.com/.
